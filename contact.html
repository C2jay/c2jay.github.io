- About Me -

My first foray into coding, admittedly unknowingly, was as a teenager formatting my livejournal. I added images and gifs, linked various pages, and worked to make an account that was as organised, readable and pretty as possible.

Classic teenager.

These days I have taken a more active role as a student of programming. I am currently a member of the She Codes Plus cohort; a six-month course that transitions women into technological careers through integrated self-paced and facilitated instructions. Projects created throughout the course teach skills such as HTML/CSS, Python, React, Django and JavaScript. Below are projects that demonstrate my abilities in the above languages. As a former English/ESL teacher and writer, I aim to synthesise my written, organisational and interpersonal abilities with my growing technical skills to become an asset to my new workplace.
